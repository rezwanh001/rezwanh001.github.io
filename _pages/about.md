## Bio

**Md Rezwanul Haque** is an incoming PhD candidate in the [Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering (ECE)](https://uwaterloo.ca/electrical-computer-engineering/) at the [University of Waterloo](https://uwaterloo.ca/), Canada. He recently completed his **MASc in ECE** at the [Centre for Pattern Analysis and Machine Intelligence (CPAMI)](https://uwaterloo.ca/centre-pattern-analysis-machine-intelligence/) Lab under the supervision of [Prof. Fakhri Karray](https://uwaterloo.ca/scholar/karray) and [Prof. Pin-Han Ho](https://uwaterloo.ca/electrical-computer-engineering/profile/p4ho). He will pursue his doctoral research under [Prof. Fakhri Karray](https://uwaterloo.ca/scholar/karray) and [Prof. Zhou Wang](https://ece.uwaterloo.ca/~z70wang/), focusing on **Multimodal Machine Learning**, **Large Language Models (LLMs)**, and **Agentic AI Systems** for human-centered applications.

His research spans multimodal depression detection, continuous sign language recognition, medical image analysis, and embodied AI — published in top-tier venues including [**ICCV**](https://iccv2025.thecvf.com/), [**IEEE SMC**](https://ieeesmc2025.org/), [**IJCNN**](https://2025.ijcnn.org/), [**IEEE Access**](https://ieeeaccess.ieee.org/), and [**Elsevier**](https://www.elsevier.com/). He is the recipient of the <a href="https://openaccess.thecvf.com/ICCV2025_workshops/MSLR" class="award-link">ICCV 2025 Best Poster Award</a>, <a href="https://openaccess.thecvf.com/ICCV2025_workshops/MSLR" class="award-link">MSLR-2025 Challenge Winner (2nd Place)</a>, and multiple <a href="https://uwaterloo.ca/engineering/" class="award-link">Faculty of Engineering Awards</a> at UWaterloo.

Prior to his graduate studies, he worked as a **Machine Learning Engineer** and **AI Consultant**, accumulating professional experience in deep learning systems and real-world AI deployment. He holds a [**BSc in Computer Science and Engineering**](https://www.kuet.ac.bd/cse) from [Khulna University of Engineering & Technology (KUET)](https://www.kuet.ac.bd/), Bangladesh.

His upcoming PhD research will focus on **Agentic Systems for Embodied AI**, **Multi-Agent Embodied Systems with Shared Visual Understanding**, and **World Models & Visual Cognitions for Embodied Agents**. To view the full CV, <a href="/cv/" class="cv-link-btn">click here</a>.
